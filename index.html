Youngeun Lee

Narrative 3D Environment Artist

Turning Imagination into Structure, Narrative into World

Born in Ulsan in 2003, she developed a unique perspective by bridging the gap between logic and narrative. A self-driven creator with a passion for world-building, she integrates the precision of Art & Technology with the soul of Creative Writing. Rather than follwing a single path, she has spent her formative years synthesizing these two disciplines, seeking to design virtual environments where stories ar not just told, but lived.
